Hello! I'm Ivar.

I have always been passionate about STEM, mathematics and new technologies in particular. But it wasn't until I graduated high school I realized that I wanted to combine my technical skills with my interest in business and society to solve real, business related problems. That insight is the reason why I now, almost 5 years later, am about to graduate from KTH with a M.Sc. in Industrial Engineering and Management.

For fall 2020 I'm considering jumping into a range of careers. Wherever I end up, I'll make sure that I have the opportunity to apply my analytical skills in my daily work, preferably in a data-driven environment. It is important for me not to lose touch with my technical side, although I wouldn't want to be in an environment where I didn't see the results of my work. The application of new technology is what motivates me.

To be concrete, my main technical interests revolve around the application of optimization, which could range all the way from data science and machine learning to system design. On the business end I tend to appreciate analytically related tasks, including things like operational efficiency as well as business intelligence.
